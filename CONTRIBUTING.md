# Contributing

When contributing to this repository, please follow these guidelines:

1. **Stay on topic:** Ensure your contributions are relevant to the project. All interactions should follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
2. **Strive for quality:** Proofread your changes for clarity, spelling, and grammar to ensure they are easy to understand.
3. **Follow the process:** Submit your changes via a pull request. Your contribution will be reviewed, and if it’s a good fit for the project, it will be merged.
